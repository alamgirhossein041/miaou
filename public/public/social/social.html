With the donate feature on My Pet Social, creators can earn extra income on our platform by receiving tokens from their fans/supporters. In addition, we will update the interactive functions between fans and creators so that everyone can express their feelings through Emoji icons.
Communication between all NFTs gaming owners

By using NFT tokens, My Pet Social allows staking to be more dynamic! Instead of being tied to a user’s wallet address when staking, it is linked to a transferable NFT.
